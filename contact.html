❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: When is the best time to visit Ninh Binh?

A: The best time is spring (March-May) or autumn (September-November) when the weather is pleasant, not too hot, and not too cold.

Q: How long do I need to see the main attractions?

A: 2-3 days is ideal to see the main sites like Trang An, Tam Coc, Hoa Lu, and Bai Dinh Pagoda. If you have more time, you can also explore Cuc Phuong National Park.

Q: Is Ninh Binh safe for tourists?

A: Ninh Binh is a safe tourist destination. However, like any city, you should maintain good hygiene and keep an eye on your personal belongings.

Q: What currency is used in Ninh Binh?

A: The official currency is Vietnamese Dong (VND). International credit cards are accepted at large hotels and restaurants, but cash is preferred at small establishments.

Q: Do I need a visa to visit Ninh Binh?

A: This depends on your nationality. Vietnamese citizens don't need a visa. Foreign visitors should check visa requirements with the Vietnamese embassy in their country.

Q: Can I rent a motorbike or car in Ninh Binh?

A: Yes, you can rent motorcycles, cars, or bicycles at many places. However, if you're not familiar with local traffic, we recommend hiring a tour guide or joining a tour.

Q: What budget should I plan for a 2-3 day trip?

A: It depends on your preferences:

  • Budget: $85-130 (budget hotels, local restaurants)
  • Mid-range: $220-350 (3-star hotels, standard restaurants)
  • Luxury: $435+ (upscale hotels and fine dining)

Q: Should I book tours in advance or upon arrival?

A: Booking in advance is more convenient as you'll have a confirmed itinerary. However, you can also book tours upon arrival, though prices may be higher and availability might be limited.

Q: How is Ninh Binh different from Ha Long Bay?

A: Ninh Binh is called the "Ha Long Bay on land" because it has similar scenic beauty but on land with limestone mountains. Ninh Binh also has richer historical heritage with many historic sites.
