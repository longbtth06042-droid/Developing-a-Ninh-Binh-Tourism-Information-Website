Famous Tourist Attractions

Ninh Binh is home to unique and wonderful tourist attractions. Below is a list of the main attractions you shouldn't miss when visiting Ninh Binh.

🏔️ Trang An - Halong Bay on Land

Trang An is a complex of beautiful limestone mountains lying on flat terrain, creating a unique landscape similar to Halong Bay but on land. Trang An was recognized by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site in 2014.

Main activities: Boat tours on the Ca River, exploring caves, enjoying panoramic views.

Opening hours: 7:00 - 17:30

Admission: 250,000 VND (boat tour)

Tam Coc - Three Caves

Tam Coc is a famous scenic spot with three natural caves and the Ca River flowing through. You can sit on boats to admire the caves.

Price: 200,000 VND

Mua Caves

Mua Caves stands alone on a high rocky mountain. Visitors can climb to the peak to enjoy breathtaking views of the entire Trang An area.

Price: 150,000 VND

Hoa Lu - Ancient Capital

Hoa Lu is the ancient capital of the Dinh and Pre-Le dynasties. This site preserves valuable historical relics including temples dedicated to ancient kings.

Price: 100,000 VND

Bai Dinh Pagoda

Bai Dinh Pagoda is Vietnam's largest temple with many large Buddha statues. It's an important spiritual pilgrimage site.

Price: 100,000 VND

Hang Mua Cave Pagoda

Hang Mua Cave Pagoda is a natural cave with a large temple altar, combining natural scenery with Buddhist spirituality.

Price: 50,000 VND

Cuc Phuong National Park

Cuc Phuong is Vietnam's oldest national park with diverse wildlife and plant species. A paradise for nature lovers.

Price: 200,000 VND

Son Dong Cave

Son Dong Cave is a natural cave with beautiful architecture and complex cave systems, a paradise for exploration.

Price: 150,000 VND

Cat Island - Photography Paradise

Cat Island is an ideal photography destination with beautiful views of the river and surrounding mountains.

Price: Free (0 VND)

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1: Trang An (boat tour) → Mua Caves (mountain climb) → Bai Dinh Pagoda

Day 2: Tam Coc → Hoa Lu → Hang Mua Cave Pagoda

Day 3: Cuc Phuong National Park (if time permits)
