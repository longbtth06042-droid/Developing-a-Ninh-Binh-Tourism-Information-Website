Local Cuisine of Ninh Binh

Ninh Binh is famous not only for its beautiful landscapes but also for its rich culinary tradition with unique local dishes. Delicious rice dishes, aromatic broths, and fresh vegetables from the Ninh Binh region will leave a lasting impression on your palate.

🍜 Local Specialty Dishes

Cơm Tấm (Rice with Ribs)

Cơm Tấm is one of Ninh Binh's most famous specialties. Made from broken rice grains, it has a wonderful aroma and is served with pork ribs, pate, egg, and stir-fried spinach.

Price: $1.50 - $2.20

Ca River Shrimp & Fish

Shrimp and fish from the Ca River are prepared in multiple ways: grilled, fried, braised, or in soup. The seafood is fresh and the broths are rich and flavorful.

Price: $3.50 - $6.50/kg

Ca River Crab

Fresh river crabs cooked in tomato sauce or steamed with lime leaves. The crab meat is sweet with a distinctive broth.

Price: $4.50 - $9/kg

Grilled Goat Ribs

Roasted or grilled goat is a luxury dish in Ninh Binh. The meat is tender and not gamey, served with fish sauce and fresh herbs.

Price: $6.50 - $11/kg

Roasted Quail Eggs

Roasted quail eggs are a popular snack. The eggs are roasted over charcoal, with a crispy shell and creamy yolk that is very appealing.

Price: $0.10 - $0.15/egg

Pickled Gourd (Dưa Chi)

Pickled gourd is a preserved vegetable with a tangy, salty flavor. It's commonly served with rice or porridge.

Price: $0.25 - $0.50/plate

Bánh Canh (Tapioca Noodles)

Bánh Canh is made with a fragrant bone broth, served with shrimp, crab, or pork. The noodles are chewy and delicious.

Price: $1.30 - $1.75/bowl

Grilled Chicken with Fish Sauce

Grilled chicken with fish sauce is a Ninh Binh specialty. The chicken is golden and aromatic, best served with hot rice.

Price: $5 - $8/chicken

🐔

Gà Nướng Nước Mắm

Gà nướng nước mắm cay là một đặc sản của Ninh Bình. Gà được nướng đến vàng ươm, ăn kèm cơm nóng rất hấp dẫn.

Giá: 120,000 - 180,000 VND/con

🍴 Famous Restaurants

Ba Truc Cơm Tấm Restaurant

An extremely famous rice restaurant on Hoa Lu ancient street. The rice is perfectly cooked, the ribs are crispy, and prices are reasonable.

Price: $1.75 - $2.60/serving

Location: Hoa Lu Ancient Street

Ca River Seafood Restaurant

Restaurant serving fresh seafood from the Ca River. Spacious and airy atmosphere with beautiful river views.

Price: $6.50 - $13/person

Location: Ca River Bank Road

Uncle Tong's Bánh Canh

Traditional bánh canh restaurant with aromatic bone broth, tender noodles, and abundant toppings.

Price: $1.50 - $2.20/bowl

Location: Doi Can Street

Anh Thinh Grilled Goat

Famous grilled goat restaurant with golden, tender goat meat that has no gamey smell and is absolutely delicious.

Price: $8.70 - $13/kg

Location: Tran Hung Dao Street

Trang An Panorama Café

Café with panoramic views of Trang An, peaceful atmosphere, delicious drinks, and tasty pastries.

Price: $1 - $1.75/cup

Location: Dong Trang An

Ca River Crab Hotpot

Hotpot restaurant with fresh crabs from the Ca River, delicious broth, cozy atmosphere, and attentive service.

Price: $11 - $17.50/person

Location: Tran Phu Street

🥘 Regional Specialties Around Ninh Binh

Phu Quoc Fish Sauce: Although not produced in Ninh Binh, Phu Quoc fish sauce is an essential condiment in local cuisine.

Thanh Hoa Clams: Clams are served at many restaurants, prepared in various ways, and are incredibly crispy and delicious.

Bac Ninh Country Pork: Large markets supply country pork from Bac Ninh with tender, high-quality meat.

💡 Dining Tips

✓ Breakfast: Try cơm tấm or bánh canh at local restaurants

✓ Lunch: Sample fresh seafood or roasted goat at restaurants

✓ Dinner: Enjoy crab hotpot or grilled chicken at famous establishments

✓ Afternoon: Coffee or tea at cafés with beautiful views
